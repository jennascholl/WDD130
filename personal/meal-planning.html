My Meal Planner

Maybe you’re the kind of person that cooks dinner on a whim. If you’re not, you can use our meal planner. This allows you to save up to a week’s worth of recipes for your upcoming dinners while automatically compiling a shopping list you can print and take with you to the grocery store. All you have to do is use the recipe generator and look for meals you like. When you find something you want to make, just select the Calendar icon and pick which day you want to plan it for. If you change your mind, it’s as easy as clicking the Remove button or dragging it to a different day. Feel free to skip a day if you need to, there’s no minimum number of recipes. Just plan for what you want to eat. When you’re done, simply push Print Shopping List to get a physical compilation of all the ingredients you’re going to need for the week on one sheet of paper, quantities included.
