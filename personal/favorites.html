My Favorites

You’ve already been told that you won’t need to keep recycling the same boring recipes anymore if you use this website; this doesn’t mean you can’t make the same thing twice if you want to, though. Using the Favorites page, you can save your favorite recipes and make them again as many times as you want, whenever you want. Just click "Add to Favorites" on any recipe to archive it for later. When you navigate to the Favorites page, you will find a list of all your favorite recipes, including the title and a photo for easy recognition. Just tap on any recipe to find all the same information you’ll need in order to have another delicious homemade meal.
